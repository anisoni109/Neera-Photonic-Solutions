Photonic Solutions

We are experts in delivering cutting-edge photonic solutions, specializing in III-V semiconductors and Silicon Photonics. Our capabilities cover the entire lifecycle, from fundamental research and device-level modeling to complete photonic circuit-level modeling and the final, tested, and co-packaged end product.

  • Active Devices: Lasers (DFB, DBR), Modulators (EAM, MZM), SOAs, APDs, PIN Diodes.
  • Passive Devices: Waveguides, Couplers, MUX/DEMUX, Resonators, Grating Couplers.
  • Full lifecycle management: from R&D to final product.
  • Outsourced fabrication, testing, and co-packaging.

Optical Sensors

We design and develop custom optical and fiber-optic sensors for a wide variety of applications, with specialized expertise in gas sensing. Our sensors offer high sensitivity, precision, and reliability in challenging environments.

  • Custom sensor design for gas sensing and other applications.
  • High sensitivity and reliability for industrial and scientific use.
  • Solutions for harsh and demanding environments.
  • Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) and Fabry-Perot based sensors.

Optomechanical Design

The performance of an optical system is critically dependent on its mechanical housing and stability. We provide optomechanical design and analysis to ensure your system is robust, stable, and manufacturable.

  • Mechanical integration and precision alignment tolerances.
  • Thermal management and structural analysis (FEA).
  • Packaging design for optical and electronic components.
  • Prototyping and optimization for volume production.

Circuit and system-level design on silicon, silicon nitride, and III-V platforms. Active devices: lasers, electro-optic modulators, SOAs, APDs, PIN diodes. Passive components: waveguides, couplers, resonators. Co-packaged optics (CPO) for data centers and AI.

Optical Sensors & Systems

Fiber-optic and photonic sensor design: Fabry-Perot, Bragg grating, Mach-Zehnder, interferometric sensors. Temperature, pressure, strain, acceleration, LiDAR, distributed sensing. Full system architecture and signal processing.

System Integration

Simulation & Circuit Optimization
